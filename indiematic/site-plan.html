Overview

Purpose

The purpose is to inform people of fun indie games people can try out, and encourage the creation of more games. Also allow people to compare reviews of different games.

Audience

My intended audience is people who are teens to young adults. Primarily want to attract people interested in video games and interested in trying new ones. People interested in indie games, or retro styled games.

Dynamic elements

Review button is present under each game, when pressed takes you to a page where you can leave a review. Review is listed under game after submitted. Search bar is present to search by title or genre. When review is submitted it records that review and score on the site, the review will stay as long as you are on the site.
